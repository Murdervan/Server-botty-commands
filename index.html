💰 Casino Games

  • !bal — Check your balance or another user's balance
  • !drop — Random drops for members with the '@Casino' role — Max 3 per hour
  • !scoreboard — See who the big winner is
  • !blackjack — Play blackjack with betting. Usage: !blackjack 100 and react 👊 to Hit, ✋ to Stand, 💰 to Double Down, ✌ to Split
  • !roulette — Play roulette with betting. Usage: !roulette 50 red or !roulette 100 black or !roulette 20 green
  • !coinflip — Flip a coin with betting. Usage: !coinflip 100 (choose via reactions red or blue)
  • !addmoney — Add coins to a user. Usage: !addmoney @user 1000 (admin only)

🎮 Games & Quiz (no betting)

  • !roll — Roll a dice. Usage: !roll 6 or your number (it will be between 1 and 6 or your number)
  • !fakenews — True/False quiz. Answer with ✅ or ❌ (5 questions)
  • !flagquiz — Name the flag! 10 rounds
  • !memory — Emoji memory game (Simon)

😂 Fun & Social

  • !birthday — Usage: !birthday 25-12-2000 to register your birthday
  • !reverse — Reverse your text. Usage: !reverse hello world
  • !overdriv — Exaggerate a sentence. Usage: !overdriv this is amazing
  • !roast — Light roast (FOR FUN). Use !roast @user or !roast to roast yourself
  • !systemmsg — Random system message

🔍 Search & Information

  • !gameinfo — Get Steam game information. Usage: !gameinfo Minecraft
  • !invite statsbot — Get the Live Stats on your server

📜 Info

  • !remind — Set a reminder. Usage: !remind 5m Take a break or !remind 1h Meeting
  • !joinleaveinfo — Show join/leave statistics for the server
  • !roleinfo — Show info about a role. Usage: !roleinfo Member
  • !serverinfo — Show info about the server
  • !userinfo — Show info about a user. Usage: !userinfo @user

⏰ Misc

  • !helpme — Show this help menu with all commands
  • !helpadmin — Show admin-only commands
